CS 310


Discrete Computational Structures

Finite and discrete algebraic structures relating to computers; sets, functions, relations. Machine-oriented logic. Combinatorial problems and algorithms. Finite automata and formal language theory. *** Prerequisite: MATH 110 or 105, 122, 221, and CS 210 *** * Note: It is recommended that students take STAT 160 or STAT 200 in addition to the prerequisite before attempting CS 310. *
